Our Story

Rollover the timeline

Highlight 1

September, 2008
Jenny + Dustin first laid eyes on one another at a student leadership BBQ at Seattle Pacific University. With the scent of lighter fluid in the air, the flames of attraction were instantaneous. Unfortunately, Jenny would prove to be a tad…elusive…on the road to a dating relationship.

Highlight 2

April, 2009
On a rainy spring evening in Seattle, with Norah Jones playing ever so softly in the background, Dustin nervously asked Jenny to be his girlfriend. The first kiss followed shortly after…

Highlight 3

June, 2010
Jenny + Dustin made a wonderful journey to Scotland together. It was on this trip they decided tea times should be institutionalized in the United States and that they wouldn’t mind having a pet sheep someday.

Highlight 4

July, 2010
Jenny moved to Spokane, Washington for grad school (i.e.) Jenny + Dustin embarked upon a two-year long-distance relationship. They endured tedious drives across the state, bad skype connections, and a few tearful goodbyes (on Jenny’s end), but they survived and decided to get married, soooooo it all worked out.

Highlight 5

June-Aug 2011
Jenny + Dustin had the pleasure and privilege of taking TWO family vacations with the Feldman and Lehmann clans: they socialized with sea turtles in Kauai and made pleasantries with mosquitoes in Minnesota.

Highlight 6

January 22nd, 2012
Dustin flew to London to surprise Jenny and propose to her on the banks of the Thames River, just steps from Westminster Abbey. She said yes, and they hopped happily back across the pond to begin the adventure of future wedded bliss.

Highlight 7

January 23rd, 2012
Wedding stress began...

More Event Info

The ceremony will begin at 5:30pm and will take place outside, weather permitting. Dinner and dancing will follow shortly after. Bring your party pants/dresses people, it’s going to be a good ole’ time. Dress is semiformal; bow ties and/or bolo ties are encouraged.

Lord Hill Farms is located in Snohomish, about 45 minutes north of Seattle. Please allow between 45 and 60 minutes travel time to reach Lord Hill Farms if leaving from Seattle (it’s awkward being late to a wedding, isn’t it?). Ample parking will be available on site.

Beer and Wine will be served at the reception by a licensed bartender. If you are over the age of 21 and wish to partake, please make sure to bring your ID with you.

If you are joining us from out of town, you may wish to book a hotel reservation. Some hotels we can recommend that are located closer to the venue include:

• Guesthouse Inn & Suites Monroe ($)
• Hilton Garden Inn Seattle/Bothell ($$)
• Willows Lodge Woodinville ($$$)

Many other lodging accommodations are available closer to downtown Seattle; we recommend you look for lodging there if you’re planning on exploring the city a bunch over the weekend.

Any questions, comments, concerns, playlist requests, or accommodation inquiries, feel free to contact Jenny or Dustin: JennyplusDustin@gmail.com
